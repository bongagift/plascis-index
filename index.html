Our Mission

Residential construction is one of the last frontiers for full-scale automation. At Plascis, we are closing the gap between digital intelligence and physical assembly.

Our mission is to develop the neural frameworks and computer vision systems required to make residential building fully autonomous. We believe that by solving the complexities of the construction site through ML, we can increase the speed, safety, and accessibility of housing globally.

Plascis is where high-level research meets the job site. We are building the brains behind the machines that will build our future.
